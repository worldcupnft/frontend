How to Play?

An NFT is a digital asset that can be in the form of in-game assets but also can be found as memes and GIFs. Players own the NFTs they earn in games and can further exchange them or later sell them when the items reach rarity and become more valuable. One of the NFT’s purposes is to preserve the value of objects related to a gaming collection.

NFTs have unique cryptographic tokens that cannot be exchanged with other non-fungible tokens. In addition, each NFT has metadata inside that cannot be changed on the blockchain. This metadata recorded inside the NFT is like a certificate of authenticity and describes what it represents, the history, and the transaction record. Non-fungible tokens can be developed to keep value exceeding the game they were created. As a result, blockchain-built games can extend gaming economies dramatically, induct new gaming categories, and fuel the development of new game.
